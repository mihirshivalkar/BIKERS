ABOUT US

My Ride is the most affordable bike rental company in India. We specialize in motorcycle rental, from short-term motorbike rental to long-term hiring or leasing, from bike tours to bike maintenance and service we have it all. We have a wide range of geared, non-geared two wheelers and scooters for rent. We aim to be become one of Indias leading bike rentals because we simplified the process and made it affordable. All you have to do is make up your mind and pick one!
